General Dentistry

The foundation of a healthy smile. Regular check-ups catch problems early before they become expensive. We do thorough exams, digital X-rays, and professional cleanings that remove build-up your toothbrush cannot reach. We also provide fluoride treatments, sealants for kids, and nightguards for grinding.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Your smile is the first thing people notice. We offer porcelain veneers, dental bonding, and full smile makeovers designed to look completely natural. Every cosmetic treatment starts with a consultation so you can see exactly what to expect before we start any work.

Restorative

Dental Implants

The gold standard for replacing a missing tooth. Implants look, feel, and function like natural teeth. Unlike bridges or dentures, implants do not shift or require special cleaning. With proper care, they last a lifetime. We handle the full process from placement to crown in our office.

Ask about implants →

Orthodontics

Orthodontics

Straighter teeth at any age. We offer clear aligners for adults and teens who want to straighten without the metal look, as well as traditional braces for more complex cases. Treatment planning includes photos, scans, and a full preview of your projected result before you commit.

Cosmetic

Teeth Whitening

Professional whitening delivers results that over-the-counter strips cannot match, in a fraction of the time. We offer in-office whitening for same-day results and take-home trays for gradual whitening on your schedule. Results typically last 12 to 24 months with proper maintenance.

Emergency Care
