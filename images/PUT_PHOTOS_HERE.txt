Drop your clinic photos into this folder.

Recommended files to add:
  clinic-hero.jpg    — Reception or treatment room photo for the Home page hero (800x600px)
  about-doctor.jpg   — Doctor headshot for the About page (400x500px)
  team.jpg           — Full team photo for the About page (optional)

Then update the HTML file to point to your image:
  Replace: <!-- <img src="images/clinic-hero.jpg" ... /> -->
  With:    <img src="images/clinic-hero.jpg" alt="Our dental clinic" />
